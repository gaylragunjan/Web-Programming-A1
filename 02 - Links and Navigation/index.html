Synopsis

Welcome to the world of the unknown, where frogs sing and the leaves are always a golden orange. A whimsical yet menacing forest full of fairytale-like wonder. Brothers Wirt and Greg are clueless on how they ended up in this strange world, and yearn to go back home. As they venture through the woods, they encounter peculiar creatures, and even stranger human beings, learning lessons on life, and death, while uncovering the many secrets of the mysterious Unknown.

WIRT

The main protagonist and the series' self-appointed contrarian. An epitome of a pessimist, his seriousness and disdain for the world gets them in trouble a lot, despite being the older of the two. He thinks of himself as a wise know-it-all, only to forget he is also just a naïve young boy entering a realm of something he's never known before.

GREG

A witty boy with overflowing creativity and ideas, always acting on impulse with the widest grin to be seen in the Unknown. His innocence and endless fascination with the woods does him and his brother more favors than Wirt's seriousness ever could. He's quite fond of wandering, making songs, and making friends along the way!

BEATRICE

The tritagonist and the brothers' tour guide to the Unknown. Introduced as a talking bluebird but later revealed to be cursed by a pastor. She is closed off and calculated, and talks with sprinkles of sarcasm even in her good-hearted advice.

THE WOODSMAN

A grieving father, desperate to bring his dearest daughter Anna back to life. Ordered by the Beast to chop edelwood so that his daughter's soul stays lit in his dark lantern, he is reserved, determined, and a loving family man who wanders the Unknown in quiet torment.

THE BEAST

The main antagonist. An ominous entity that lurks the Unknown and is feared by all its inhabitants. In order to survive, he needs edelwood tree sap made from children's souls like Wirt and Greg's. He is not ready to accept death yet.
